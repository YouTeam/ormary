UK Fashion And Textile Awards 2014
// By Merita

The winners of the NatWest UK Fashion & Textile Awards 2014 were announced last night at a star-studded event in East London's Tobacco Dock. The evening started with a catwalk show featuring designs by all of the nominees, before leaders in the industry were recognised for their achievements. Guests were invited afterwards to participate in a silent auction that raised a total of £19,000 for the chosen charity, Save the Children.

Christopher Raeburn became a UKFT award winner for the third time as he received the UKFT Menswear Brand accolade, having previously picked up the UKFT Rise Award in 2012 and the UKFT Designer Business Award at last year's ceremony. Notable brands House of Holland, Alex Monroe and Margaret Howell were all recognised with awards, as was Matchesfashion.com for retail.

For the first time, the public were invited to vote for their favourite designer collaboration in the newly launched MyDaily UKFT Designer Collaboration Award. Isabel Marant pour H&M was chosen as the winner of the coveted prize.

See the full list of winners below:

  • House of Fraser UKFT Rise Award
  • Okun Beachwear Ltd.
  • UKFT Accessories Award
  • Alex Monroe Ltd.
  • MyDaily UKFT Designer Collaboration Award
